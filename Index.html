Arden Thornburg

Spanish and TESOL Instructor / Instructional Designer

I am a Spanish Instructor for Kansas City Kansas Community College and Metropolitan Community College, in the Kansas City Metro area. Additionally, for the past two years I have worked as a TESOL/ESL Instructor for the Independence School District. Prior to that, I worked as a graduate teaching assistant, and later instructor, at the University of Missouri-Kansas City. For nearly a decade I have worked with, and guided, high school, college, and adult learners in their desire to learn a new language. I hope to use this experience as a catapult for my endeavors in the Instructional Design and Technology field.
